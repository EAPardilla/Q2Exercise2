Science Topics

Momentum

Momentum is the product of an object's mass and its velocity. It is the object's quantity of motion. It is a vector quanity and its direction is the same as its velocity. The SI unit of momentum is kg*m/s.

Valence Electrons

Valence electrons are electrons found on the valence shell or the outermost shell of an atom.

Force

Force is the push or pull on an object. It is an external agent capable of changing a body's state of rest or motion. Force has a magnitude and direction. Its SI unit is Newtons.
